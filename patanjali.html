Patanjali Yogpeeth Haridwar

Patanjali Yogpeeth Haridwar is a Naturopathy Center, aimed at providing psychological and spiritual health to people suffering from various incurable diseases and stress through integrated therapy of Ayurveda in the environment with fresh and pure air, verdant and vibrant nature. While Naturopathy rejuvenates body with its various therapies which are given in peaceful environment in the heavenly nature, that further heals soul of individuals from stress and worries.
After successfully delivering holistic well-being through our flagship institutes Yoggram and Niramyam, Patanjali Wellness brings to you another world-class wellness center at haridwar. This center will be the first ever largest wellness center after yoggram and niramayam which will provide fusion of, Naturopathy (the ancient Indian art of healing), Ayurved (Herbal medicine) Yoga (mind body balance), Panchkarma therapies (Body Detox) acupressure , acupuncture and yagya therapies.
